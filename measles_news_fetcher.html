At a glance

Why measles still matters

  • 93% protection after one measles-containing dose
  • 97% protection after two doses
  • 1,362 confirmed U.S. cases reported by CDC in 2026 as of March 12
  • 95,000 estimated global measles deaths in 2024

Method

How this works

This page reads a scheduled JSON feed generated from news search results. Headlines are deduplicated, checked for recency, and grouped by source type.

It is meant to be a monitoring tool, not a formal systematic review. In other words, useful for signal, not the final word.
