How to Play

  • You start with 600 floating question marks.
  • You lose at 1000 question marks. You win when all the question marks are gone.
  • Correct answers reduce the question marks by 25 percent. Wrong answers increase them by 50 percent.
  • The quiz adapts to your answers and gets harder as you do well.
  • The questions get harder, and the time to answer gets shorter.
  • The very last question mark disappears only if you answer a hard-level question correctly.
  • You can answer with the mouse or by pressing 1, 2, 3, or 4.
