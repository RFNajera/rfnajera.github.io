How to Play

  • You start with 600 germs.
  • You lose at 1000 germs. You win when all germs are dead.
  • Correct answers reduce the colony by 25 percent. Wrong answers increase it by 50 percent.
  • The game will adapt to your answers and get harder as you answer correctly.
  • The questions will get harder, and the time to answer will get smaller.
  • The last remaining germ is killed only by answering a hard-level question correctly.
  • You can answer with the mouse or by pressing 1, 2, 3, or 4.
